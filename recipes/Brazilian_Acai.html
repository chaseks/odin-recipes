Acai

Description

Acai bowls are made from frozen acai palm fruit which is pureed and typically served in a bowl with granola, fruit and guarana syrup.
However, Brazil is a huge country and then cusine varies grealty from region to region with European, African, Asian, and native cuisines
influencing this meal. Generally speaking, acai is made savory in the north and sweet in the south. Acai bowls are incredibly popular in Rio De Janeiro.
Other than beack kiosks, you can also find them in juice/smoothie joints, gym cafeterias, or at a mall food court.

Ingredients

  • Packets of frozen Acai Berry+Guaran puree
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Banana
  • Kiwis
  • Granola

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar and water in a cup
  2. Add frozen packets of acai to a bledner and add the water/sugar
  3. Blend well and pour the mixture into a bowl
  4. Top with sliced fruits and granola
